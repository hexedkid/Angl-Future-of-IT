Topic 6: Sustainable IT (Green Tech)

IT's Role in Environmental Responsibility

As technology proliferates, so does its environmental footprint. **Sustainable IT (Green Tech)** is a growing discipline focused on designing, manufacturing, using, and disposing of computing resources with minimal environmental impact. The IT industry is committed to becoming a solution to, rather than a cause of, climate change.

Key Focus Areas:

  1. **Energy Efficiency:** Optimizing Data Centers to reduce power consumption (e.g., using renewable energy, implementing advanced cooling techniques).
  2. **Circular Economy:** Promoting the longevity of hardware, responsible recycling (e-waste reduction), and refurbishment.
  3. **Green Software Engineering:** Writing code and building applications that require less computational power and energy to execute.

This transition involves utilizing AI to optimize energy grids and logistics, making IT the crucial enabler for a globally sustainable future. **Ethical and Environmental responsibility** is rapidly becoming a core pillar of modern technological development.
