Topic 5: Metaverse and XR (VR/AR)

The Next Evolution of the Internet

The **Metaverse** represents the next generation of the internet—a persistent, shared, virtual 3D space where users can interact with digital objects, other users, and the environment in real time. It is an evolution built on **Extended Reality (XR)** technologies.

XR is an umbrella term covering all immersive technologies:

  • **Virtual Reality (VR):** Fully immersive digital experience (e.g., gaming, virtual training).
  • **Augmented Reality (AR):** Overlaying digital information onto the real world (e.g., filters, maintenance manuals projected onto equipment).
  • **Mixed Reality (MR):** Combining real and virtual worlds to produce new environments where physical and digital objects coexist and interact.

**Impact:** Beyond gaming and entertainment, the Metaverse and XR are transforming education (virtual classrooms), engineering (digital twins for factories), and remote work (virtual collaboration spaces), promising a new era of digital presence.
